How it works

Three steps between a receipt and a settled group

No spreadsheets, no chasing people in group chats. Create a group once, then let SplitEasy do the arithmetic every time someone pays for something.

Step 01

Create a group

Start a group for your trip, flat or team, then invite people with a link or a scannable QR code. Only invited members can join.

Step 02

Add an expense

Log what was paid and by whom. Split it equally or by custom shares — the admin decides who else is allowed to add expenses.

Step 03

Settle up

See exactly who owes what on the analytics page, then pay or get paid directly over UPI. No more mental math after the bill arrives.

Why SplitEasy

Everything a group needs, nothing it doesn't

Built around how groups actually share expenses — one admin, clear permissions, and numbers everyone can trust.

Admin-controlled groups

Group admins manage members, set who can add expenses, and can remove or dissolve the group at any time.

Invite by link or QR

New members join only through a private invite link or a QR code the admin shares — never an open, searchable group.

Clear analytics

A dedicated dashboard shows exactly how much you owe and how much others owe you, updated the moment an expense is added.

Fair, flexible splits

Split evenly or set custom shares per person — SplitEasy handles the calculation and keeps a running record for the group.

Real-time notifications

Get notified the moment you're added to a group, an expense is logged, or someone settles a payment with you.

Built for UPI

Save your UPI ID on your profile so settling a balance with a groupmate is one tap away, no separate app needed.
